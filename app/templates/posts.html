Kyrecce Lisbie Welcome Post

27/06/2026

Here is a look at some of Kyreece Lisbie's last season stats compared to one of his teammates, Harry Anderson, and a player similar in position and league finish, Calum Agius of Crewe Alexandra

First we look at Goals and Assists where Lisbie is close to his Harry, and is ahead of Calum by a decent amount

Next we look at the shots of each player, in which we can see Lisbie is ahead of both players in all values, showing how he could be consistent in getting shots off and getting them on target

When looking at the players passing, we can see that Lisbie is behind on all passing values, suggesting that he might be more of an attacking winger who focuses on getting down the pitch rather than building up play

Finally, Looking at some attacking stats its clear that Lisbie exceeds in getting forward with the ball, with more dribbles and a lot more touches in the opposition box, once again suggesting that Lisbie is best played as a high line attacking midfielder

Overall, I think this a brilliant signing for both the club and Leam. He should be a worthy investment who could be sold for a decent amount and also suits Leam's philosophy of fast wingers who get into the box and get shots off
